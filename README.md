# A.R.C. (Autonomous Rotating Core)

A.R.C. is a high-stakes minimalist action-survival game that challenges your reflexes and spatial awareness. You control a scanning probe orbiting a collapsing singularity, fighting against entropy to stabilize a fractured timeline.

## 🕹️ Core Gameplay Mechanics

* **Orbital Survival:** Your probe is locked in a circular orbit around a central singularity. You must avoid incoming debris and "Bad" entities while collecting data fragments.
* **The Reverse Tap:** Control is simple but deep. Tap the screen (or Spacebar) to instantly reverse your orbital direction. Mastering this "U-turn" is the only way to navigate crowded fields.
* **Orbit Decay:** The singularity constantly pulls you in. Your orbital radius shrinks over time. If you reach the center, the signal is lost.
* **Stability Streaks:** Collecting green data fragments in rapid succession builds your "Sync." Reaching high Sync levels triggers advanced operational modes.

## 🚀 Advanced Game Modes

* **Dual-Core: Infinity Pulse:** Triggered by maintaining a perfect collection streak. Your orbit transforms from a circle into an Infinity Symbol (∞) trajectory. In this mode, you toggle between two gravitational centers, allowing for unique defensive maneuvers and high-speed collection.
* **Chaos Mode: Reality Fracture:** A high-risk event where the singularity destabilizes. Projectiles are fired rapidly from the center, and the environment enters a state of flux. High danger, but high reward.
* **Overdrive (Victory Lap):** Stabilize the core to level 10 to trigger Overdrive. All remaining "Bad" entities are ejected from the center, turning into harmless points as you ride out the explosion of a White Hole.

## 🎨 Immersive Experience

* **Dynamic Visual Stress:** As your orbit shrinks, the UI begins to glitch. You'll see red-shifted scanlines, feel increased screen vibration, and witness decay particles streaming toward the void.
* **Soundscape:** Every collection and collision is accompanied by procedural synth tones. Each power-up has a distinct "sonic signature," from the celestial choir of God Mode to the glitchy sawtooth of a System Failure.
* **Story Integration:** Randomly decoded terminal logs appear on-screen, hinting at the crumbling reality you are trying to save (e.g., "THE VOID IS LISTENING", "TIMELINE FRACTURED").

## 🏆 Progression

Survive as long as possible to increase your System Level. Each level increases the speed and density of entropy, pushing your piloting skills to the limit. High scores are saved locally through the A.R.C. Storage System.
